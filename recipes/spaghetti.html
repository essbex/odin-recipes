Spaghetti

Description

So, this is a suuuupper simple recipe.

Everyone should know how to make themselves a nice big plate of Spaghetti, so let me help you!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Garlic (a bunch!)
  • Onion (1 should suffice)
  • Green Bell Pepper (1)
  • Olive Oil (just a drizzle)
  • Tomato Sauce (2 cans)
  • Diced Tomatoes (1 can)
  • Tomato Paste (1 can)
  • Black Olives (1 can)
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Tarragon

Steps

  1. Chop up the garlic, onion, and green bell pepper
  2. Drizzle some oil in a pot, and add the veggies. Saute.
  3. Add the tomatoes to the pot and stir well.
  4. Add the oregano, basil, red pepper, and tarragon to you liking
  5. Let simmer for 15 minutes
  6. Meanwhile, bring a big pot of water to boil. Add noodles and cook as directed
  7. Add olives to the tomato sauce.
  8. Ladle noodles onto a bowl, and top with tomato sauce.
  9. Enjoy!
