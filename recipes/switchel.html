Switchel

Description

A super refereshing drink that's also p good for you - Who knew?!?

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of ginger
  • 3 3/4 cups water
  • 2 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 Tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Place ginger in one cup of water in a small pot and bring to a boil
  2. Pour ginger water, remaining water, Apple Cider Vinegar, lemon juice, and honey into a jar.
  3. Stir, and enjoy!
